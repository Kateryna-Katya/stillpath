About the Game

Big Farm: Merge Game transforms merging into a farming journey.
Players start with basic resources and gradually unlock more complex production systems.
Every little thing you do somehow makes your farm a bit better - it all adds up.

How To Play

Combine, harvest, expand

  • Merge Resources

    Combine identical items to create better tools and crops.

  • Unlock Production

    Develop new chains for farming and crafting.

  • Complete Tasks

    Follow objectives to guide farm growth.

  • Expand Land

    Open new areas and increase capacity.

  • Optimize Output

    Improve efficiency through smarter merging.
